Making Tea.

1.Boil Water in a pot.

2.Add tea leaves and sugar.

3.Let it simmer for a few minutes.

Making Paratha and Aloo Curry.

1.Knead the dough for paratha and let it rest.

2.Roll out the dough and cook on a hot griddle with oil or ghee.

3.For aloo curry,saut onions,tomatoes and spices in a pan.

4.Add boiled potatoes and cook until the curry thickens.

Making Biriyani.

1.Marinate the meat or vegetables with spices and yogurt.

2.Cook basmati rice until its 70% done.

3.Layer the marinated meat/vegetables and rice in a pot.

4.Cook on low heat (dum) until fully cooked
